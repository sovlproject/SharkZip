About SharkZip

SharkZip is a simple, powerful utility designed to protect your privacy. Easily compress folders and files into encrypted ZIP archives. With a clean interface and robust encryption, your data stays safe from prying eyes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I zip a folder or file? Tap the "Zip Folder" or "Zip File" button on the main screen, select your item from the Files app, set your secure password, and you're done!
How do I view my zipped files? Tap "My Zips" to see all your encrypted archives. From there, you can share them or open them in the Files app.
Is my data sent to any servers? No. SharkZip processes everything locally on your device. We never see your files or your passwords.
