FAQ

How do I zip a folder or file? Click the "Zip Folder" or "Zip File" button on the home screen. Select your item from the file picker, enter your desired password, and SharkZip will generate your encrypted archive instantly.
How to view zipped files? Tap the "My Zips" button to see your history. You can tap any file to preview it, share it with others, or save it back to your device's Files app.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy is our top priority. **SharkZip does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

1. Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information, device identifiers, or usage statistics. SharkZip does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

2. Data Processing: All file compression and encryption (ZIP creation) are performed locally on your device. Your files and passwords never leave your iPhone/iPad and are never uploaded to any servers.

3. Data Storage: SharkZip stores zipped files in the app's local sandbox directory. You have full control over these files and can delete them at any time by removing them from the "My Zips" list or uninstalling the app.

4. Security: We use standard encryption protocols provided by the ZipArchive library to ensure your files are protected by the password you provide. We do not have access to your passwords.

5. Contact: If you have questions about this policy, contact us at the email address listed above.
