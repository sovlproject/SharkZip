Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

The Short Version: SharkZip does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data, files, or passwords. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

SharkZip is designed to be a "Zero-Data" utility. We do not collect personal information, email addresses, device identifiers, or IP addresses. There is no account registration required to use the app.

2. File Processing

All file compression (zipping) and encryption are performed locally on your iPhone or iPad. Your files and the passwords you set for them are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase) or advertising SDKs. No tracking of any kind is performed within the app.

4. Data Storage

Files created within the app are stored in the app's internal sandbox. You can delete these files at any time. When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed by the operating system.

5. Contact Us
